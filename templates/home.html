History


Christian Muller Women's College(CMWC) established by the North Kerala Diocese of the Church of South India (CSI) is a response to a longlasting need of the people of Chombala and Vadakara area for higher education.It was the Basal Evangelical Missionary Society, or Basal Mission which started working at the Malabar Coast,that brought basic education and opened it's doors of learning to the backward classes and communities in Malabar.
CMWC will be a self-financing Institution and will aim at imparting high quality education in Arts and Science and will now offer graduate level programmes in subjects have a ready demand in market.


  • Established:2013
  • Affilated to:University of Calicut

COURSES


BCA

BCA(Bachelor's In Computer Application) is a 3-year undergraduate degree programme that focuses on knowledge on the basis of computer application and software development.

BA ENGLISH

BA ENGLISH(Bachelor's of Arts) is a 3-year undergraduate degree programme that focuses on English Literature, cultural studies, the history of English literature.

BCOM

BCOM(Bachelor's In Commerce) is a 3-year undergraduate degree programme that focuses on knowledge on the basis of Analytical skills, Financial Literacy, Business Laws.

BSC Psychology

Bachelor In Science is an undergraduate course with a duration of 3-years.This course explores the interactions between the human mind,behaviour and experience.

Msc.Psychology

MSC psychology is a 2-year postgraduate course that focuses on the practical application of research methods related to human behaviour.
